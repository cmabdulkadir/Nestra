BIO

Nësträ is a 22 yr old singer and songwriter. Born in Minneapolis, Minnesota and raised in Seattle, Washington where he currently resides. Nësträ first got into music when he was in highschool. His highschool had a radio statation that had a studio where he got inspired to create. It was then when decided to give 100 percent to his music career. Not only is Nësträ into music but he also into fashion. Since then he has performed at several events and festivels in Seattle. He was also mentored by macklemore which gave Nësträ an opportunity to be a part of Fashiion Week NYC representing the Seattle Sounders. Some of his inspiration when it comes to music were Chris Brown, Mario and Micheal Jackson. Music to him is a way of expressing emotions and he strives to deliver that in each of his songs.
